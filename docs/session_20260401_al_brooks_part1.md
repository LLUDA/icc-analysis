### New Insights Extracted

#### 1. Market Reality: 50/50 Most of Time
- Brooks states market is 50/50 most of trading day
- 60% probability during trends
- 70%+ during spikes
- **ICC 78% WR comes from ONLY trading high-probability setups**

#### 2. Trend Bars vs Doji Bars
- Trend bar = one side in control
- Doji = balance between bulls/bears
- **ICC Continuation should occur during/after trend bars, not dojis**

#### 3. Cumulative Pressure Concept
- Buying/selling pressure accumulates
- Each bull body in correction = buying pressure
- **ICC Correction with many small bars = weak pressure = lower probability**

#### 4. 80% of Reversals Fail (CRITICAL!)
> *"The market is always trying to reverse, but 80 percent of those reversal attempts fail and become bull flags."*

- **ICC = Trading when reversal FAILS**
- ICC 78% WR aligns with Brooks' 80% failure rate
- ICC Correction = the "failed reversal attempt"
- ICC Continuation = entry when trend resumes

#### 5. Institutional Activity
- Institutions drive price action
- ICC = piggyback trading on institutional activity
- Institutions defend entries = SL at structure is protected

#### 6. Measured Moves & Fibonacci
- Brooks: "Fibonacci are just approximations"
- **Use structure levels, not Fibonacci levels**
- Chart patterns that stand alone > Fibonacci

#### 7. Climax Patterns = ICC AVOID
- After consecutive large trend bars
- After parabolic moves
- **Don't enter ICC after climax - trend may reverse**

#### 8. Two-Legged Pullbacks = ICC Foundation
- Brooks: "Two-legged corrections are reliable setups"
- A = Initial move, B = Pullback, C = Continuation
- ICC = Brooks' ABC pattern!
